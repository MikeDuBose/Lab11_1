Who I Am

I'm a thirty year old United States Navy veteran just trying to make my way in the world. I am currently lucky enough to be employed by Quicken Loans as an Associate Software Engineer in Training. I am currently half-way through the training program put forth by Quicken Loans. I currently enjoy hanging out alone and about 6 feet away from other humans. I enjoy plenty of recreational activites that are legal in Michigan including skydiving, disc golfing, Animal Crossing, and camping.

Where I'm From

I'm from nowhere, really. Both of my parents (and their parents before them) joined the Navy in the mid-eighties. With both of them serving in active duty, we generally moved around every 2 years or so; so when someone asks me where I'm from, I usually tell them "Detroit", even though I'd like nothing more than to leave this city.

Where I'm Going

After this completes, I'm looking forward to landing a spot on an actual development team and getting to work!
